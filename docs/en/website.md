# Web control (Chinese beta)

TinyPlay can open Bilibili, iQIYI, Tencent Video, and Youku on the computer connected to your TV, then let you control the page from your phone. You must sign in yourself and follow each service's rules for accounts, memberships, regions, and supported devices.

## How to use it

1. Choose a website from **Website library** on your phone.
2. The website opens on the computer connected to the TV.
3. Return to **Remote** on your phone to control playback, seek, change speed, or enter and exit fullscreen.

This feature turns web controls on the computer into a remote that works better from the sofa. It is currently a Chinese beta and supports only the four websites listed above. Website redesigns may occasionally require TinyPlay's controls to be updated.

TinyPlay does not remove ads, bypass sign-in, membership, device, or regional restrictions, or provide downloading, recording, rebroadcasting, or stream URL extraction. The computer must use the service in the same way it normally would.
